# About LIT

## Introduction
The Laboratory for Information Transmission (LIT) conducts research on both the theoretical analysis and practical design of transmission technologies for modern wireless communication systems. Our current focus is on core technologies for sixth-generation (6G) mobile communications that integrate artificial intelligence, communication, and sensing.

## Vision & Goal
LIT has been designated as a University ICT Research Center (ITRC) by the Ministry of Science and ICT. Our goal is to secure original, **AI-based Integrated Sensing and Communication (ISAC)** technologies for 6G mobile communications.

## Research Areas

**Integrated Intelligence, Sensing, and Communication (I²SAC)**
- Extended target estimation in near-field ISAC
- Robust beamforming for ISAC
- Semantic and token-based communication
- Multi-modal AI-based channel estimation
- OTFS-based ISAC waveform design

**MIMO and Massive MIMO**
- Robust hybrid beamforming
- Deep unfolding-based WMMSE precoding
- Terahertz-band beamforming

**Network Performance Optimization via Deep Reinforcement Learning**
- Task offloading for cell-free MEC
- Multiple-target tracking using multiple UAVs
